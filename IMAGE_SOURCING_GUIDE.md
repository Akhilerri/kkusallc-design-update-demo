# Image Sourcing Guide for Hotel Website

## Overview
This guide explains how to source appropriate images for the Karan Kothari USA LLC website, focusing on hotel FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment) and hospitality imagery.

## Image Sources

### 1. Hotel Brand Websites (Recommended)
Source professional images directly from major hotel brand websites:

#### Hilton
- Website: https://www.hilton.com
- Press/Media Center: https://newsroom.hilton.com/media-library
- Look for: Lobby furniture, guest rooms, restaurant seating, fixtures

#### IHG (InterContinental Hotels Group)
- Website: https://www.ihg.com
- Press Center: https://www.ihgplc.com/en/news-and-media
- Look for: Hotel interiors, FF&E installations, brand-specific furniture

#### Marriott
- Website: https://www.marriott.com
- Newsroom: https://news.marriott.com
- Look for: Property images, furniture details, design elements

#### Hyatt
- Website: https://www.hyatt.com
- Press Room: https://newsroom.hyatt.com
- Look for: Hotel photography, interior design, furniture showcases

### 2. Unsplash (Free Stock Photos)
- Website: https://unsplash.com
- Search terms:
  - "hotel lobby"
  - "hotel furniture"
  - "luxury hotel interior"
  - "hotel room"
  - "restaurant furniture"
  - "hospitality design"

### 3. Pexels (Free Stock Photos)
- Website: https://www.pexels.com
- Similar search terms as Unsplash

## Image Requirements

### Technical Specifications
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 500KB when possible)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for hero images, flexible for gallery

### Content Guidelines
✅ **DO Use Images Showing:**
- Hotel lobby furniture and fixtures
- Guest room furniture (beds, desks, chairs)
- Restaurant and dining furniture
- Lighting fixtures and decorative elements
- FF&E installations in hospitality settings
- Professional, high-quality hotel interiors
- Brand hotel properties (Hilton, IHG, Marriott, etc.)

❌ **AVOID Images With:**
- People's faces (unless from official brand sources)
- Competitor branding or logos
- Low-quality or amateur photography
- Residential interiors (we supply hotels, not homes)
- Generic office furniture
- Overly staged or unrealistic setups

## Copyright & Usage

### Hotel Brand Images
- ✅ Use for reference and inspiration
- ✅ Use press/media images with proper attribution
- ⚠️ Check each brand's media usage guidelines
- ⚠️ Avoid using images that show competitor products

### Stock Photos (Unsplash/Pexels)
- ✅ Free to use for commercial purposes
- ✅ No attribution required (but appreciated)
- ✅ Can be modified and edited
- ✅ Safe for website use

## Image Optimization

Before uploading images to the website:

1. **Resize** to appropriate dimensions
2. **Compress** using tools like:
   - TinyPNG (https://tinypng.com)
   - ImageOptim (Mac)
   - Squoosh (https://squoosh.app)
3. **Rename** descriptively: `hilton-lobby-furniture.jpg`
4. **Add alt text** for accessibility
